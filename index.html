About Our Daycare

At Rina's Daycare, we are dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment for children to learn and grow. Our philosophy is rooted in a child-centered approach, where we foster curiosity and creativity through a balanced curriculum of play and structured learning.

Our experienced and passionate educators are committed to building strong partnerships with parents, ensuring a seamless and supportive experience for the entire family. We believe that every child is unique and we celebrate their individuality, helping them to develop essential skills for a bright future.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs tailored to the developmental needs of children at every stage of their early education journey.

Infants (6 Weeks - 12 Months)

Our infant program provides a warm and nurturing environment for your baby's first year of learning and discovery.

Toddlers (1 - 2 Years)

Toddlers explore the world through sensory play, language development, and the beginning of social interactions.

Preschool (3 - 5 Years)

Our preschool curriculum prepares children for kindergarten with foundational skills in literacy, math, and social-emotional growth.
